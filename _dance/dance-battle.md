<p>Registration: On-spot open registration</p>
<ul>
<li>Adapt to the beats of the changing music.</li>

</ul><br>

<br>

<ol>
    <li>Adaptation to the beats and pace of the music</li>
    <li>Spontaneity </li>
    <li>Entertainment Value</li>
    <li>Choreography</li>
</ol>
